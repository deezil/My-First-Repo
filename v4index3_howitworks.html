How Hirehive Works

We're empowering storytellers everywhere with the Sharing Economy. This is Hirehive. The world's first Peer-to-Peer rentals platform for filmmakers.

  • Hiring Gear

  • Listing Gear

List Your Gear

It's totally free to list your gear on Hirehive. Our Listing Tool guides you through the simple listing process.
And once you're ready to put your gear to work, you can publish your listing for our community to see.
(You can always edit your listing details anytime you like.)

Communicate

Filmmakers within our community (Hivers) will want to book your gear! Use our messaging tool to communicate with them, answer any questions they may have and sort out the hire details.

You've Got Control

As a Hive Host, you have total control over pricing, availability and who hires your gear.
When you receive a Booking Request, choose whether to accept or decline the request within 24 hours, or it will expire.
It's best if you respond to every request, even if it's just to decline it. Leaving someone hanging just ain't a good look.

Gear Pick-up & Drop-off

After you accept a Booking Request and your Hiver makes payment, it's time to make the gear exchange and check the gear with your Hiver.
We provide our members with printable Gear Checksheets for you to use during Pick-up and Drop-off.

Get Paid

We release payment to your nominated account 24 hours after the end of your hire. Money should hit your bank in 3 to 5 days. All our payments are secure and powered by our friends at Stripe.

Rate & Review

Both you and your Hiver will rate and review each other after each transaction. This lets our community know about the awesome experiences you're having with other members. Positive reviews help build your Hirehive Reputation.

Find Gear

Quickly find the gear you need by searching our listings. Search by brand, type and location.
No more spending hours on the phone or guessing about pricing.

Make Contact

Message Gear Owners (Hive Hosts) by clicking on 'Request a Booking' on their listing page.
This creates a Booking Request where you can ask any question you may have, and confirm availability.
Your Hive Host will have 24 hours to respond before a request expires.

Book

Once you've worked out all the details with your Hive Host, all that's left to do is to make payment and book the gear!
Rest easy. All payments on Hirehive are done securely through our friends at Stripe.

Gear Pick-up & Drop-off

Now it's time to make the gear exchange and check the gear together with your Hive Host. We all know how much care needs to go into handling gear.
We provide our members with printable Gear Checksheets for you to use during Pick-up and Drop-off.

Time to call 'Action'!

You've picked up the gear and it all checks out. It's now time to get shooting! We can't wait to see the stuff you've produced!

Rate & Review

Both you and your Hive Host will rate and review each other after each transaction. This lets our community know about the awesome experiences you're having with other members. Positive reviews help build your Hirehive Reputation.
