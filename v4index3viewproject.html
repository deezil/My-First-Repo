Fell
Brisbane, QLD, Australia

  • activity

  • credits

  • press

FELL is a beautiful story about the importance of human connection. It explores the themes of love and loss against the desolate and barren wastelands at the end of the world. It examines the idea of love and how it is the one thing that truly binds us together, that forces us to keep going, to keep living. Even when all hope is lost in the world, love is what will bring us out of the darkness.
