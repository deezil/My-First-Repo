About Us

Here's to the storytellers of our generation.

You're the reason for our being.

Dedicated to storytellers everywhere.

You're the reason for our being.

You inspire us to make things better.

 

We believe that by bringing the Share Economy to filmmakers, we can help filmmakers tell the stories they're compelled to tell. Without compromise.

 

Through Hirehive, gear owners can become micro entrepreneurs in their own right. Earn money to finance your next project, justify buying that next piece of your kit or go for that well deserved vacation while your gear makes money for you.

 

Meanwhile, filmmakers can now get greater access to awesome gear at affordable prices. Connect with owners to hire their gear while making new connections with fellow filmmakers.

 

We are very excited about the possibilities that Hirehive may bring, and we can't wait to share Hivehive with you.

 

Venture forth, oh Creative One! This is your call to adventure!

 

The Hirehive Founders, Brent & Dee
